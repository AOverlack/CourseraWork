The data set "TidyData-Means" contains the average values of the means and standard deviations of the measurements by the Samsung smartphone. These apply to:
Linear accelaration along X,Y and Z axis
Gyroscopic acceleration along the X,Y and Z axis
Gravity accelaration
Further description of the way the data were collected and their meaning is obtainable here:
http://archive.ics.uci.edu/ml/datasets/Human+Activity+Recognition+Using+Smartphones 

All observations in the rows are split per combination of Activity and Subject:
In total 30 subjects (Numbered 1-30) have performed the measurements and measurements concern 6 Activities: (Walking, Walkig up stairs, walking down stairs, sitting, standing an laying. )

The various mean-values of measurements are indicated by the remaining column headers.
